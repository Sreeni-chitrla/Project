Best Time To Visit, March to June

Manali best time to visit The charm of Manali lies in its snow-capped mountains, scenic landscapes, and misty valleys. The best time to visit Manali is between March to June, when the temperature hovers between 10°C to 25°C. This period is considered the best time to visit Manali as the weather is pleasant, and you can enjoy various adventure activities like paragliding, river rafting, and trekking
